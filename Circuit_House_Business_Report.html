Circuit House

Business Report on an E-commerce Gadget Store

Prepared by the Group Members

Business Report: Circuit House

Introduction

This report presents the planning, development, and early progress of our proposed e-commerce business, Circuit House. Circuit House is an online store designed to sell laptops, laptop accessories, phones, speakers, fans, and other electronic gadgets. The idea behind the business is to create a convenient platform where customers can find useful technology products without necessarily visiting a physical shop.

In recent years, e-commerce has become a very important part of business because many customers prefer to search, compare, order, and pay for products online. This is especially true for electronic gadgets, because buyers often want to check specifications, prices, pictures, and reviews before making a decision. Our group therefore saw an opportunity to create a business that would not only sell gadgets, but also provide reliable information and good customer service.

Circuit House is being built around trust, convenience, affordability, and easy access. The business will focus on products that students, office workers, business owners, households, and young people use regularly. These include laptops for school and office work, phones for communication, speakers for entertainment, fans for comfort, and accessories such as chargers, earphones, laptop bags, power banks, and phone cases.

Week 1: Business Idea Selection and Naming

During the first week, the group members met to discuss possible e-commerce business ideas. Various suggestions were raised by different members. Some members suggested fashion and clothing, others suggested cosmetics, food items, phone accessories only, and general household items. Each idea was discussed based on demand, cost, competition, ease of marketing, and the ability of the group to manage the business successfully.

After several discussions, the group agreed that an e-commerce business focused on laptop sales, laptop accessories, phones, speakers, fans, and other gadgets would be the best option. This decision was made because electronic products are useful in everyday life and are needed by many categories of people. Students need laptops and phones for learning, research, assignments, and communication. Workers and business owners use laptops and phones to perform office tasks, communicate with clients, and manage records. Accessories such as chargers, earphones, laptop bags, power banks, and phone cases are also products that people buy often.

The group also considered the growth potential of the business. A laptop and gadget e-commerce business does not have to depend on only one product. Circuit House can begin with a few fast-moving products and later expand into more electronic gadgets as demand increases. This made the idea flexible, practical, and suitable for an online market.

On that same day, after settling on the business idea, the group members discussed possible names for the business. Several names were suggested, but after careful consideration, the group finally agreed on Circuit House. The name was chosen because it connects naturally with electronics and technology.

The word Circuit represents electrical systems, devices, and the world of technology. It immediately gives customers the idea that the business deals in gadgets and electronic products. The word House suggests a place where many related items can be found. Together, Circuit House gives the impression of a trusted place for electronic devices and accessories.

The name is also simple, professional, and easy to remember. This is important because a business name should be easy for customers to pronounce, type, and recognize on social media, flyers, and websites. The group wanted a name that would sound modern and suitable for an online gadget store, and Circuit House matched that purpose.

By the end of Week 1, the group had successfully chosen the business type, settled on laptop and gadget sales as the main focus, and selected the business name Circuit House. This gave the group a clear direction for the remaining weeks of the project.

Week 2: Assignment of Roles and Business Setup

During the second week, the group focused on assigning roles and setting up the basic structure of the business. This was done to make the work more organized and to ensure that every important part of Circuit House received attention. Assigning roles was necessary because a business project involves many tasks, and it would have been difficult for everyone to handle everything at the same time.

The sharing of duties helped the group work faster and more responsibly. Each member understood what he or she was expected to do, and this reduced confusion. It also encouraged teamwork because every person had a specific contribution to make toward the success of Circuit House. The roles assigned included bank account creation, SIM card registration, website development, logo and flyer design, enquiries from wholesalers and manufacturers, and other business-related research.

Bank Account Creation

One important role was the creation of a business bank account. A bank account is necessary because it allows the business to receive payments from customers, keep business money separate from personal money, and maintain proper financial records. It also makes the business look more serious and trustworthy.

The group chose Stanbic Bank for the business account. Stanbic was selected because it is a well-known and respected bank with a strong reputation. Many customers feel safer when they are asked to pay into an account from a recognized bank. This can help build confidence, especially because Circuit House will operate mainly online.

Another reason for choosing Stanbic is its reliable digital banking services. Since Circuit House will receive online payments, it is important to use a bank that supports quick transfers, mobile banking, and easy confirmation of transactions. This will help the business respond to customers faster after payment has been made. Using Stanbic also gives the business a more professional image and supports better financial management.

SIM Card Registration

Another assigned role was SIM card registration. The business needs an official phone number that customers can use to contact Circuit House. This number will be used for calls, WhatsApp messages, payment confirmations, order enquiries, delivery updates, and general customer support.

Having a separate business SIM card is important because it separates personal communication from business communication. It also makes the business easier to manage. Customers will be able to save one official number and use it whenever they need assistance. This helps the business appear organized and reliable.

Website Development

Website development was also assigned to some members because the website is the main online space for Circuit House. Since the business is an e-commerce business, customers should be able to visit the website to view products, check prices, read product descriptions, and contact the business to place orders.

The website will make Circuit House look more professional than a business that only depends on social media posts. It will also help customers browse products at their own convenience. A customer who wants to buy a laptop, for example, can compare different options on the website before contacting the business.

During the subsequent days, the group also created a blog website. The purpose of the blog is to support the business by providing useful information. The blog can contain articles such as how to choose a good laptop, how to take care of a phone battery, the benefits of using power banks, and the best accessories for students. This will help customers make better decisions and also attract more people to the Circuit House brand.

Logo and Flyer Design

The design of a logo and flyers was also assigned to members. A logo is important because it represents the identity of the business. It is what customers will easily recognize on social media pages, product flyers, receipts, and the website. The Circuit House logo is expected to communicate technology, simplicity, and trust.

Flyers are also necessary because they will be used to advertise products and promotions. A flyer can include product pictures, prices, contact details, payment options, and delivery information. Well-designed flyers can attract attention quickly, especially when shared on WhatsApp status, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and TikTok. Since customers often judge an online business by how professional its posts look, good design is very important.

Enquiries from Wholesalers and Manufacturers

Some members were assigned to make enquiries from wholesalers and manufacturers. This role was very important because Circuit House needs reliable suppliers who can provide quality products at reasonable prices. Without good suppliers, the business may face problems such as high costs, poor-quality goods, and delayed supply.

The members responsible for this role contacted and researched wholesalers and manufacturers to find out the prices of laptops, phones, speakers, fans, and accessories. They compared prices from different sources so that the group could understand the cost of the products and decide on suitable selling prices.

This research helped the group understand the market better. It showed which products are affordable, which products are likely to sell faster, and how much profit the business could make. It also helped the group avoid guessing prices. Good pricing is important because if prices are too high, customers may not buy, and if prices are too low, the business may not make enough profit.

Other Enquiries and Research

Other members were responsible for general enquiries and research. This included finding out delivery options, checking competitor prices, studying customer needs, and looking at how similar online gadget businesses operate. This role was useful because it helped the group make informed decisions instead of depending only on assumptions.

Through these enquiries, the group gained a better understanding of how to position Circuit House in the market. The research also helped the group think about customer service, product presentation, delivery fees, and the type of online content that would attract buyers.

By the end of Week 2, the group had moved from simply having an idea to creating a working structure for the business. Members had clear duties, and important setup activities such as the bank account, business contact line, branding, website planning, and supplier research had been started.

Week 3: Product Research, Website Development, Payment and Delivery Planning

During the third week, the group concentrated on the main operational parts of Circuit House. This included product research, website development, blog creation, payment planning, and delivery planning. At this stage, the business idea became more practical because the group started working on how customers would actually find products, make payments, and receive their orders.

Products and Services

Circuit House will sell a variety of electronic products and accessories. The main products include laptops, laptop chargers, laptop bags, phones, phone chargers, phone cases, earphones, headphones, speakers, power banks, fans, and other useful gadgets.

Laptops are one of the major products because they are needed by students, workers, business owners, and professionals. Students use laptops for assignments, online classes, research, and presentations. Workers use them for typing documents, meetings, emails, reports, and other office activities. Business owners also use laptops to manage records, communicate with customers, and handle online transactions.

Phones are also important because they are used by almost everyone. People use phones for calls, messaging, internet browsing, mobile money transactions, social media, and entertainment. Accessories such as chargers, power banks, earphones, and phone cases were included because they are fast-moving products and are purchased more often than major gadgets.

Speakers and fans were added because they serve practical needs. Speakers are useful for entertainment, events, personal use, and small businesses. Fans are also useful in homes, offices, shops, and hostels. By selling different products, Circuit House can serve different categories of customers and increase its chances of making regular sales.

Supplier and Price Enquiries

During this week, the group continued and completed enquiries from wholesalers and manufacturers. This was important because the business needs reliable suppliers who can provide quality products at reasonable prices. The group compared prices for laptops, phones, speakers, fans, and accessories so that selling prices could be planned properly.

This research helped the group understand the market better. It showed which products are affordable, which ones are likely to sell faster, and how much profit the business could make. It also helped the group avoid guessing prices. Good pricing is important because prices that are too high can drive customers away, while prices that are too low can reduce profit.

Website and Blog Development

Website development was also continued in Week 3. The website is important because it serves as the main online space for Circuit House. Customers should be able to visit the website to view products, check prices, read product descriptions, and contact the business to place orders.

The group also created a blog website to support the business. The purpose of the blog is to provide helpful information to customers. The blog can contain articles such as how to choose a good laptop, how to take care of a phone battery, the benefits of using power banks, and the best accessories for students. This will help customers make better decisions and also attract more people to the Circuit House brand.

The blog will make the business look more knowledgeable and trustworthy. When customers see that the business provides useful information, they may feel more confident buying from it. The blog can explain product features in simple language and help customers understand what they are buying before they spend their money.

Mode of Payment

Circuit House will use simple and convenient payment methods to make transactions easy for customers. The main payment methods will include mobile money, bank transfer, and cash on delivery where necessary.

Mobile money will be one of the most important payment options because it is widely used and very convenient. Many customers already use mobile money for daily transactions, so it will be easy for them to pay for products through their phones. Mobile money also allows the business to confirm payments quickly and process orders faster.

Bank transfer will also be accepted through the Stanbic Bank account. This option is suitable for customers who prefer paying directly into a bank account, especially for more expensive items such as laptops and phones. Bank transfers also help the business keep proper records of money received.

Cash on delivery may be allowed for nearby customers or trusted customers. This means the customer pays when the product is delivered. However, the group understands that this method must be handled carefully because some customers may place unserious orders. For this reason, cash on delivery may be limited to selected areas or used only after proper confirmation.

Delivery Method

Delivery is an important part of Circuit House because the business will operate mainly online. Customers who buy products online expect their items to be delivered safely, quickly, and in good condition. After a customer places an order and payment is confirmed, the product will be packaged properly and delivered to the customer.

For customers within nearby areas, delivery can be handled by a group member or a trusted dispatch rider. This will help reduce delays and make communication easier. For customers in distant locations, the business can use courier services or trusted transport delivery services. Delivery charges will depend on the customer's location, the size of the product, and the delivery method used.

The group understands that reliable delivery can help build customer trust. If customers receive their products on time and in good condition, they are more likely to buy again and recommend Circuit House to others. For this reason, proper packaging, delivery confirmation, and customer communication will be taken seriously.

By the end of Week 3, the group had made strong progress in understanding product prices, supplier options, website development, payment methods, and delivery arrangements. These activities helped turn Circuit House into a more realistic and workable business plan.

Week 4: Marketing Strategy, Challenges and Future Aspirations

During the fourth week, the group focused on how Circuit House would attract customers, handle possible challenges, and grow in the future. Since the business will operate mainly online, marketing is one of the most important parts of the plan. The group agreed that social media would be used as the main marketing tool because it is affordable, fast, and able to reach many people within a short time.

Marketing Strategy

Marketing will play a major role in the success of Circuit House. Since the business is online, the group decided to use social media as the main marketing tool. Social media is affordable, fast, and able to reach many people within a short time. Platforms such as WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and TikTok will be used to promote the business and communicate with customers.

WhatsApp

WhatsApp will be used because it is one of the most common communication platforms. Many people check WhatsApp every day, so it is a good place to share product pictures, prices, flyers, and updates. WhatsApp status can be used to advertise new arrivals, discounts, and available gadgets. WhatsApp groups can also help spread information quickly. Customers can easily chat with the business, ask questions, and place orders directly.

Facebook

Facebook will be useful because it has a large number of users and supports business pages, marketplace posts, and groups. Circuit House can create a Facebook page where products are posted regularly. The business can also share products in buying and selling groups to reach people who are already interested in shopping. Facebook is good for reaching different age groups, including students, workers, parents, and business owners.

Instagram

Instagram will be used because it is very good for visual marketing. Gadgets attract attention when they are shown clearly through pictures and short videos. Circuit House can use Instagram posts, stories, and reels to display laptops, phones, speakers, fans, and accessories. Instagram also helps a business look modern and stylish, which is useful for a technology brand.

Twitter/X

Twitter, now also known as X, will be used because it is popular for quick updates, trending conversations, and public interaction. Many young people, students, professionals, and technology-minded users are active on the platform. Circuit House can use Twitter/X to post short product updates, promotions, customer feedback, and simple tech tips. It can also help the business join conversations about gadgets, school needs, office tools, and technology trends.

TikTok

TikTok will be used because short videos are very popular and can reach many people quickly. Circuit House can create videos showing product reviews, unboxing clips, laptop comparisons, phone accessories, and demonstrations of speakers or fans. TikTok is especially useful for reaching younger customers who enjoy quick and interesting videos. A good video can attract attention faster than a normal text post.

Expected Challenges

Like every business, Circuit House may face some challenges. One expected challenge is competition. There are already many individuals and businesses selling gadgets online, so Circuit House must work hard to stand out. The business can do this by offering good customer service, fair prices, quality products, and reliable delivery.

Another challenge may be the cost of stocking products. Laptops and phones can be expensive, so the business may not be able to buy many items at the beginning. To manage this, Circuit House can start with fast-moving accessories and a few major gadgets, then expand gradually as sales increase.

Delivery delays may also occur, especially for customers in distant locations. To reduce this problem, the business must work with reliable dispatch riders and courier services. Supplier price changes may also affect the business, so the group will need to keep checking the market and updating prices carefully.

Aspirations and Future Goals

As a group, our aspiration is to grow Circuit House into a trusted and well-recognized e-commerce brand for electronic gadgets and accessories. We do not want the business to remain only a small online project. Our aim is to build a brand that customers can depend on whenever they need laptops, phones, accessories, speakers, fans, and other technology products.

In the future, we aspire to become one of the preferred online stores for students, workers, businesses, and households looking for reliable gadgets at fair prices. We want Circuit House to be known not only for selling products, but also for helping customers make the right choices. This means providing clear product information, honest recommendations, and good after-sales support.

Another goal is to expand our product range as the business grows. At the beginning, Circuit House may focus on laptops, phones, and common accessories, but we hope to later include more advanced gadgets such as tablets, smart watches, gaming accessories, printers, networking devices, and office electronics. By doing this, the business can serve more customers and meet different technology needs.

We also aspire to improve our delivery system over time. Our goal is to reach customers in more locations and make deliveries faster, safer, and more reliable. As the business becomes stronger, we hope to partner with trusted delivery companies and possibly create a more organized delivery arrangement for regular customers.

In terms of online presence, we aspire to grow our website, blog, and social media pages into active platforms where customers can shop, ask questions, read helpful technology tips, and receive updates on new products. We want Circuit House to become a place where people do not only buy gadgets, but also learn more about how to choose and use them properly.

Our long-term aspiration is to build Circuit House into a business that can create employment opportunities and support young people with interest in technology, sales, marketing, design, delivery, and customer service. If the business grows successfully, it can provide practical experience and income opportunities for more people.

Overall, we aspire to reach a stage where Circuit House becomes a reliable technology brand with strong customer trust, wider market reach, quality products, and professional service. With commitment, teamwork, good planning, and continuous improvement, we believe the business can grow from a group project into a successful e-commerce enterprise.

By the end of Week 4, the group had developed a clearer plan for marketing, customer communication, managing challenges, and growing Circuit House beyond the first stage of the project.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the planning and development of Circuit House has been a productive group project. During the first week, the members suggested different e-commerce business ideas and later agreed to create an online store that sells laptops, accessories, phones, speakers, fans, and other gadgets. On that same day, the group chose the name Circuit House because it represents technology, electronics, and a trusted place for gadgets.

On the second day, roles were assigned to members to make the work organized and effective. These roles included bank account creation, SIM card registration, website development, logo and flyer design, enquiries from wholesalers and manufacturers, and other research. During the following days, the group created a blog website and completed enquiries about product prices from wholesalers and manufacturers.

Circuit House will use convenient payment methods such as mobile money, Stanbic Bank transfer, and cash on delivery where necessary. The business will also use reliable delivery methods to make online shopping easy for customers. Social media platforms such as WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter/X, and TikTok will be used for marketing because they are popular, affordable, and effective for reaching different types of customers.

With good teamwork, trusted suppliers, fair pricing, attractive marketing, reliable delivery, and strong customer service, Circuit House has the potential to grow into a successful e-commerce business.
