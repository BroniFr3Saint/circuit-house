Business portfolio

CircuitHouse

A student-built e-commerce technology business focused on laptops, phones, accessories, reliable delivery, budget matching, and operating system installation services.

Identity

Who we are

CircuitHouse is an online gadget store created to make technology products easier to find, compare, and buy. The brand focuses on clear prices, useful specifications, simple ordering, and customer support.

Mission

What we serve

We serve students, workers, business owners, content creators, families, and offices that need laptops, phones, speakers, headsets, fans, computer accessories, and setup services.

Payment

How customers pay

Customers can pay through mobile money, Stanbic Bank transfer, and selected cash-on-delivery arrangements for nearby or confirmed orders.

Delivery

How orders move

Orders can be picked up, delivered within Accra, sent by express dispatch, or moved through regional courier services depending on the customer location and item size.

Services

OS installation desk

The business also provides Windows, Linux, macOS recovery, ChromeOS Flex, dual boot, driver setup, backup support, and developer workstation configuration.

Aspiration

Where we want to reach

Our aspiration is to grow CircuitHouse into a trusted technology marketplace with strong customer trust, wider product reach, reliable delivery, and employment opportunities for young people.

Project strength

CircuitHouse combines product sales, budget search, delivery planning, supplier research, branding, social media marketing, and operating-system services into one practical e-commerce project.
